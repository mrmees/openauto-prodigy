## Decisions Made
- `clearClientSession()` disconnects all signals from client_ before calling abort() to prevent synchronous re-entrant signal emission (abort can fire errorOccurred/disconnected)
- Always-replace on new connection with warning log including old and new peer addresses
- Inactivity timer resets only on valid MAC-verified status messages (not on hello or theme messages) to prevent unauthenticated traffic from keeping zombie sessions alive
- Refactored stop() to delegate to clearClientSession() instead of duplicating cleanup logic
