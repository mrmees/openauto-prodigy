## Approach Pivot Note

The plan called for changing `fillMode` from `PreserveAspectCrop` to `PreserveAspectFit`. During execution, discovered the root cause was `AdditionalVideoConfig` in the SDP breaking margin negotiation. The actual fix removed `AdditionalVideoConfig` so the phone honors margins correctly — making `PreserveAspectCrop` the correct fillMode (content fills viewport edge-to-edge with no letterbox needed).

Commits: `e95219b` (initial Fit attempt) → `633fbf8` (real fix: remove AdditionalVideoConfig) → `81341b6` (black navbar override during AA)
