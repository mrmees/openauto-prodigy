<objective>
Fix AA video rendering so the navbar doesn't overlap/crop Android Auto content.

Purpose: The phone (S25 Ultra) is ignoring margin_width/margin_height in the SDP VideoConfig, causing it to render full-screen while PreserveAspectCrop cuts into the actual content at top and bottom. Changing to PreserveAspectFit makes all content visible regardless of whether the phone honors margins.

Output: AA video fully visible with correct touch alignment, no cropping.
</objective>
