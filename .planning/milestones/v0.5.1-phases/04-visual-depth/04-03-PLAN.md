   - `shadowColor: ThemeService.shadow`

5. Add state layer Rectangle inside bg (anchors.fill, same radius, `color: ThemeService.onSurface`, `opacity: mouseArea.pressed ? 0.10 : 0.0`)
6. Keep existing scale animation (0.95)
7. Keep existing primaryContainer color swap on press

**SettingsListItem.qml:**

2. Remove `opacity: mouseArea.pressed ? 0.85 : 1.0`
