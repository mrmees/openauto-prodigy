### Concerns (non-blocking)

1. **Content dimensions not recomputed on resolution change** — `onConfigChanged()` updates AA resolution but not content dimensions accounting for navbar. Touch mapping would be wrong after live resolution change until restart. Low risk (resolution rarely changes during AA).
2. **wantsFullscreen() null fallback** — Returns `true` (fullscreen) when hostContext null, inverted from intended default. Unreachable in production.
3. **EDGE-02 dynamic reflow** — Content area margins don't update live on edge change. Documented known issue, requires restart.
