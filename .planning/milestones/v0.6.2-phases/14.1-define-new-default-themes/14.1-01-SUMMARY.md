## Decisions Made

- **Prodigy as default:** Renamed prodigy to default (id: default) so ThemeService fallback loads it on fresh install
- **Fresh palette re-pull:** Initial SCP pulled a stale prodigy palette; re-pulled fresh from companion after regeneration (commit 4aa207b)
- **Wallpaper cap at 1440x1440:** All wallpapers over 1440px resized down -- Pi GPU has limited VRAM and these are background textures
- **FullScreenPicker shadow removal:** MultiEffect shadows on 9+ delegate items caused GPU freeze on Pi; removed shadows entirely rather than reducing quality
- **Old themes retired entirely:** ember, ocean, amoled removed rather than kept as legacy options -- companion-generated palettes are strictly better
