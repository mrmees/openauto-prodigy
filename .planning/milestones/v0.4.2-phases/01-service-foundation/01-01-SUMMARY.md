## Decisions Made
- Pre-flight script is generated by the installer via heredoc (not a separate tracked file) -- keeps install.sh as single source of truth
- BindsTo=hostapd is intentionally one-directional: hostapd death kills Prodigy, Prodigy death does NOT kill hostapd
- ExecStopPost=-/bin/systemctl stop hostapd runs only on final stop, not between Restart= cycles
- --check-only flag on pre-flight enables diagnostic use without self-healing side effects
